Our Courses

LLB

In LL.B. 6 semester examinations are to be cleared before the award of the degree by the University. In case of LL.B. each paper is divided in to Mid-term (20 written +10 Viva) and end-term (70 marks written) assessments!

Learn More

LLM

In LL.M, the students have to pass 4 semesters. Each paper is divided in to mid-term(30 marks) and End term(70 marks). During the course, the student will have to submit 2 dissertations and 2 PPTs as term-end evaluation (PPT in 1st and 4th & dissertation in 2nd and 3rd). The specialization course for LL.M. is Commercial Law!

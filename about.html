About Us

Welcome to Dhenkanal Law College, Dhenkanal

The Dhenkanal Law College, Dhenkanal is one of the most premier educational institutions in LAW in the state of Odisha. Its Scenic atmosphere surrounded by the beauty of nature expresses its pursuit of excellence. The fragrance of the academic smells can be realized in every corner and corridor of the .

The Classes are held in the morning from 8 A.M. to 2 P.M. on every working day except Sunday & Holidays. The College is permanently affiliated to Madhusudan Law University and permanently affiliated to the Bar Council of India, and permanently recognized by the State Government of Odisha. It has been established since the year 1981 and accredited by NAAC (An autonomous body). Bangalore.

The college is well equipped having adequate classrooms, library, Computer and hostel facilities. The college is organizing different types of Seminars, Symposiums, Moot Court and provided Para legal aid Services to achieve the better performance of students in addition to their course. Most of the teachers have doctorate degrees and having a strong base in the field of Legal Education.

The college office functions from 8 A.M to 2 P.M Admissions are made in 3yrs LL.B Course of the College after the publication of the results of the Degree examination of Utkal University. Applicants for Admission in to the 3 years Law Course of the college should have passed the examination for degree of Bachelor of Arts, Science, Commerce, Bachelor of oriental Learning, Bachelor of medical and Bachelor of Surgery, Bachelor of Science in Engineering, B. Pharm, Bol., M.A., M.Sc., M.Com. or any other examination recognized by Academic Council of M.S. Law University or equivalent there to and should have secured minimum 45% marks in the aggregate in any of the aforesaid qualifying examinations. In case of SC/ST candidates, the qualifying marks will be 40% with production of their caste certificate.

Applications for admission in to the three years LLB (CBCS) Course are to be submitted within 14 days from the date of publication of the result of Utkal University with attested copies of the marks obtained in the degree examination and the money receipt of the application form. Selections for admission will be made strictly on mark basis taking into consideration the aggregate marks of the qualifying examination. Application form will be obtained from the Law College office on payment of Rs. 600/- in person.

Application to be sent by Post require remittance of Rs. 700/- by Bank Draft on INDIAN BANK, Dhenkanal Branch in favour of “Dhenkanal Law College, Dhenkanal” with a self-addressed envelope 9” X 4” with postage stamp of Rs. 50/-. Intimation for admission will be sent through post/email/telephone. The candidates are requested to submit the following documents in original at the time of admission through registered post or in person. So as to reach the under signed within the date as intimated. No Further time shall be allowed for admission.
